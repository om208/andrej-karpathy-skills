HOW TO USE THE SYSTEM
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

QUICK START (30 seconds per pattern):

1. Look at middle candle:
   Is it TINY, SMALL, MEDIUM, or LARGE body?
   → This narrows down to 2-3 possible pattern types

2. Look at outer candles:
   Are they TINY (<0.15) or SMALL (0.15-0.35)?
   → Further narrows options

3. Look at candle 3 tail:
   Is upper_tail > lower_tail?
   → Determines if UPSIDE or DOWNSIDE pattern

4. Check middle direction:
   Is it bullish or bearish?
   → Final confirmation

5. Match to pattern table
   → Pattern identified!

THE PARADOX EXPLAINED
================================================================================

Why does NEGATIVE filtering beat POSITIVE filtering?

1. WINNERS ARE SCATTERED
   Good trades come from many different combinations:
   - Tight compression OR bullish OR SMA bottom OR up-movement
   - No single pattern dominates
   - Filtering FOR winners dilutes sample and misses combos

2. LOSERS ARE CLUSTERED
   Bad trades come from specific toxic combinations:
   - Medium compression + down-movement = ALWAYS LOSE
   - Very-tight compression = ALWAYS LOSE
   - Down-movement alone = 0-14% win rate
   - These patterns repeat consistently

3. REMOVING CLUSTERS IS POWERFUL
   Taking "not bad" trades naturally yields winners:
   - Keep all trades except toxic clusters
   - Result: 27 safe trades out of 104
   - Of those 27, 23 are winners (85.2%)
   - No complex pattern matching needed

MATHEMATICAL INSIGHT:
  - Signal selection (find winners): -20% precision (keep some losers)
  - Noise removal (avoid losers): +35% precision (remove mostly losers, keep winners)
  - The signal-to-noise ratio improves more via noise removal
